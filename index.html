About The Conference

The European Organization of Psychologists & Psychotherapists Annual Conference is the premier event for mental health professionals across Europe.

This year's conference theme is "Innovations in Mental Health: Bridging Research and Practice." We'll explore the latest developments in psychological research and how they can be applied in clinical settings to improve patient outcomes.

Join over 500 professionals from 30 countries for keynote presentations, workshops, panel discussions, and networking events designed to advance your practice and expand your professional network.
